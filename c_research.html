🧪 PHASE 3: Immune System Engineering

⚡

In Vivo CAR‑T (off‑the‑shelf)

CRISPR/LNP delivery to reprogram T‑cells directly inside patient → eliminates weeks of manufacturing & reduces cost from $500k → $10k by 2032.

Preclinical success: leukemia, sarcoma cleared in mice (UCSF 2026)

🛡️

Armored CAR‑T for solid tumors

Engineered to secrete IL-12 or checkpoint inhibitors only inside tumor microenvironment. Phase I/II trials ongoing. Expected approval for solid tumors by 2033.

💉

Personalized neoantigen vaccines

2‑week manufacturing, $5k goal · combine with checkpoint inhibitors to reach >80% long-term response in melanoma, lung, pancreatic.

🧩

Next‑gen Checkpoints

TIGIT, VISTA, TIM‑3, NKG2A inhibitors in pipeline. Quadruple combinations by 2035 → overcome resistance.

🔬 TIL therapy expansion – lifileucel approved for melanoma; optimization to serve lung, breast, cervical, colorectal by 2032. Young TIL protocols + reduced IL‑2 toxicity.

🎯 PHASE 4: Precision & Molecular Targeting

🧬 KRAS complete elimination

G12C (approved), G12D (2027), G12V (2028), Pan‑KRAS inhibitor (2032). Plus T‑cell engagers against KRAS peptides.

🛡️ TP53 restoration

APR‑246, MDM2 inhibitors → first p53‑restoring drug approval by 2034 for 50% of cancers carrying p53 mutations.

🔗 PROTACs & molecular glues

Degrade undruggable oncoproteins; clinical expansion across breast, prostate, leukemia by 2029.

🧬 ADC expansion

30+ FDA approved ADCs by 2034 (currently ~15). Enhanced linker-payload technology.

⚡ PHASE 5: FLASH‑RT & Focused Ultrasound

💥 FLASH‑Radiotherapy

Ultra‑high dose rate (milliseconds) spares normal tissue. ARPA‑H 1‑CURE program aiming for single‑procedure radiotherapy for all solid tumors by 2030.

🧲 MR‑Linac + Histotripsy

Real‑time adaptive radiation; mechanical ablation without heat. Non‑invasive treatment for moving tumors (lung, pancreas).
